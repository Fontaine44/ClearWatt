Green Hydrogen's Potential in Quebec


Hydrogen is set to play a pivotal role in the global energy transition, with the potential to abate 80 gigatons of CO₂ emissions by 2050. As an energy storage medium and decarbonization tool for industries like steel, aviation, and heavy transport, hydrogen bridges renewable energy with hard-to-abate sectors. Achieving these ambitions will require over $300 billion in investments by 2030.

Quebec, with its 32 TWh of renewable energy surplus annually, is uniquely positioned to become a hub for green hydrogen production, leveraging its abundant hydropower and industrial base.
